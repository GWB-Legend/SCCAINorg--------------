About Us

SHOUT CLIMATE CHANGE AFRICA is a Non-Governmental Organization dedicated to addressing climate change issues across Africa

Our Mission & Vision

SHOUT CLIMATE CHANGE AFRICA (SCCA) is a Non-Governmental, non-political, non-ethnical, non-racial Organization that encourages all to "SHOUT NOW OR NEVER" on climate change issues that may affect us all.

Founded in 2016, our organization works with communities, governments, and partners across Africa to implement sustainable solutions to climate change challenges.

We focus on advocacy, education, and practical projects that address environmental degradation and promote sustainable development.
